ABOUT SADHANA PAKALA

I am a city girl, born and raised in New York. I am currently in my third year at Northeastern University pursuing a major in Marketing and Graphic Design. I have explored many aspects of design and creativity, from painting and drawing to graphic art and 3D modeling. I am especially interested in branding and editorial production and I am passionate about creating a product that conveys a message through captivating visuals. When I’m not juggling ten different design projects at once, you can find me experimenting in the kitchen or reorganizing my playlists- so far, I’ve tried a new recipe every week of quarantine, and my Spotify wrapped can attest to the 80,000 minutes I spent discovering new sounds last year.
